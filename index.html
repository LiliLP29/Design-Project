What Are Some of The Different Styles of Dance?

Home Ballet Jazz Tap Hiphop Contemporary



According to the Merriam-Webster dictionnary, dancing means "to move one's body rhythmically usually to music". Dance is very versatile, in other words, there are many different styles of dance. Some people argue that dance is an art and not a sport, some argue that when dancing in a competitive scenario, it is a sport. They way I like to see it, dance is an artistic sport. A dance choreoagraphy often tells a story or a concept. In this website, you will find information about a few of the more common dance styles, see which one appeals to you the most!
